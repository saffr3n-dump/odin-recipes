Boiled Water

Description

Welcome to the culinary adventure of boiling water! This recipe takes the simplest of ingredients and turns them into a hot, steamy delight. Ideal for tea lovers or anyone looking to elevate their hydration game!

Ingredients

  • 1 pot of tap water
  • A pinch of salt (optional)

Steps

  1. Take a pot
  2. Place it under the water tap
  3. Turn the tap on
  4. Fill the pot with tap water
  5. Turn the tap off
  6. Go to the stove (try not to spill the water on the way)
  7. Place the pot on a burner
  8. Turn the heat to high
  9. Wait until the water boils
  10. Optionally add a pinch of salt, if you're feeling adventurous
  11. Don't forget to turn off the heat
