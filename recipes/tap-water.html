Tap Water

Description

Experience the magic of tap water! This simple recipe showcases the art of creating a refreshing low calorie beverage, perfect for hydration!

Ingredients

  • 1 glass of tap water

Steps

  1. Take a glass
  2. Place it under the water tap
  3. Turn the tap on
  4. Fill the glass with tap water
  5. Turn the tap off
  6. Enjoy!
